As a Registered Counsellor, I seek to provide a safe environment for you to navigate and process your personal issues in life’s many and varied relationships, while providing a platform that seeks to achieve your desired end result.

Grief and trauma counselling

Whether trying to adjust to a major life change, relationship, self-esteem issues or struggling to cope with the loss of a loved one, often all we need is someone who can help us feel less alone when dealing with these issues. The purpose of therapy is to provide you with an empathetic, non-judgmental environment in which you can explore the difficulties you are facing and also assist you in developing the tools that will ultimately allow you to figure out how best to manage your particular situation.

A little more about me

I am registered with the HPCSA and BHP, and have been in private practice for the past eleven years. In addition to my private work, I am affiliated to national Employee Wellness companies, providing group sessions and topic talks in a Corporate environment. I offer individual one-on-one sessions, as well as online sessions and counselling to larger groups. I am married, with two grown up sons and a young daughter, and am a proud North Coast local. I love the outdoors, and enjoy socialising with family and friends in our beautiful Dolphin Coast. In my spare time, I enjoy reading a good book, walking my dogs, cooking and gardening.
